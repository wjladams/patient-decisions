Hearing aids

  • Hearing aids increase the risk of ear infections by ten fold
  • One quarter of those given hearing aids do not use them or give up on using them - the main issues they report relate to:
    • The need for regular maintenance and upkeep (change in batteries and cleaning ear moulds)
    • Some people worry about the cosmetic issues related to wearing them
    • One third of people report significant long lasting or recurring ear irritation from wearing them (occasionally from infections)
    • One in a hundred people stop wearing hearing aids because of sound issues (distortion of sound or uncomfortable feedback)
