Otoscelerosis management for

Otosclerosis can be managed in 3 main ways:

  1. Watchful waiting: You do not undertake any treatment and continue with your current level of hearing impairment
  2. Hearing aids: We provide you with hearing aid(s) to help increase the volumes of specific sounds that we know you are having difficulty hearing well
  3. Stapes surgery: We undertake an operation that bypasses the stiffening in your hearing bone and restore normal hearing
