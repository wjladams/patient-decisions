Risks

This section includes all risks associated with your treatment options. Those risks break down into sections, which we describe below.
  • Hearing Risks: If you choose either Hearing Aid or Surgery there is a small chance it could hurt your hearing. These relate to possible hearing loss on the surgery side and possible hearing distortion. The details of these risks are described in the next few slides.
  • Temporary Health Risks: If you choose either Hearing Aid or Surgery there is a small chance of health issues that only last for a few days to a month at most. These relate to anesthetic and a temporary loss of taste. The details are described in the next few slides.
  • Permanent Health Risks: If you choose either Hearing Aid or Surgery there is a small chance of permanent changes to your health, namely an increase chance of ear infections or loss of balance. The details are in the next few slides.
