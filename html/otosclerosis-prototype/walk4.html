Treatment options for

  • Watchful Waiting: continue with regular check ups on your condition, but take no other action.
  • Hearing aid: increase the the level of sound reaching the ear making it easier to hear the world around you despite otosclerosis
  • Surgery: aims to remove the cause of the reduced hearing by re-modelling the three small bones in the ear
Note: Long term follow-up is not required in most people following treatment.
