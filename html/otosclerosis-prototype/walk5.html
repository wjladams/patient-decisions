Know the treatments,

Surgery

  • Surgery is done as a day case (you leave on the same day)
  • It is done under a local anaesthetic and pain free
  • Surgery requires treatment in a hospital. Surgery takes an hour but the whole process takes half a day or so.
  • Most people take a week off work
  • You will need to attend another appointment to remove the antibiotic soaked sponge 2 weeks after surgery
  • There is a rare but serious and long lasting risk that surgery could significantly reduce the hearing in the operated ear (1 in 200)
  • There is a rare but serious and long lasting risk that surgery could significantly affect your balance (1 in 200)
  • There is a common but fleeting risk of metallic taste to the tip of your tongue (1 in 5)
